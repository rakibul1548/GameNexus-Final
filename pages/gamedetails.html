Elden Ring is a fantasy action-RPG adventure set in a world co-created by Hidetaka Miyazaki, the creator of the influential Dark Souls video game series, and George R.R. Martin, author of The New York Times best-selling fantasy series, A Song of Ice and Fire.

Danger and discovery lurk around every corner in FromSoftware's largest game to-date. Traverse the breathtaking world on foot or on horseback, alone or online with other players. Hone your skills as you face fearsome enemies in a war to claim the Elden Ring.
